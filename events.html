E-Cell x Drishti IAS

E-Cell x Drishti IAS: From Engineering Dreams to Cracking Civil Services On 17th February 2025, E-Cell in collaboration with Drishti IAS hosted an engaging and insightful speaker session in the college auditorium, with a turnout of over 200 enthusiastic participants. The session was led by Mr. Prashant Sharma, a renowned educator with 20+ years of experience, recipient of three national awards, and mentor to thousands of aspirants. Known for his expertise in training and soft skills, Mr. Sharma shared invaluable insights on: • UPSC Preparation – practical strategies and the roadmap to cracking Civil Services • Balancing College with Aspirations – managing academics while preparing for competitive exams • Soft Skills Enhancement – building confidence, communication, and personality for success The event not only inspired students to aim higher but also equipped them with a blueprint for success—whether in Civil Services or other professional pursuits. It was a powerful reminder that with the right guidance, determination, and preparation, engineers too can dream big and achieve beyond conventional career paths. One mentor. One session. One opportunity. This event left a lasting impact on everyone who attended.

The Case Conqueror

Event Overview The Case Conqueror, hosted by E-Cell IGDTUW and powered by Unstop, was not just another case study competition—it turned into a battlefield of wits, strategy, and innovation. With 350+ participants from multiple colleges and disciplines, the event challenged undergraduate students to take on real-world business problems and craft bold, game-changing solutions. Participants competed against some of the sharpest minds, tested their analytical genius, and proved their ability to think like top strategists. The judging focused on clarity of problem understanding, creativity of ideas, practicality and scalability of solutions, logical presentation, and analytical depth. This ensured that the most well-rounded and impactful solutions stood out. Event Format Team Size: 2–4 members per team (unchangeable after registration) Round 1 (Online): A real-world case study was released, and teams submitted their solutions in a 7-slide PPT (including title slide). Round 2 (Online): Shortlisted teams prepared a 2-slide deck and a 3-minute video presentation based on their solutions. Rewards & Prizes Winner: Team Bulbasaur – ₹5,000 + Certificate First Runner-Up: Team Vyapari – ₹3,000 + Certificate Second Runner-Up: Team Case Crusaders – ₹2,000 + Certificate All Participants: Certificates of participation ✨ The Case Conqueror concluded with great enthusiasm, showcasing the spirit of innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving among undergraduate students nationwide.

Orientation’25

The Orientation Session of E-Cell IGDTUW was held on 28th August, marking the beginning of an exciting new tenure of entrepreneurial activities on campus. With 100+ enthusiastic attendees, the event provided students with an opportunity to immerse themselves in the culture of innovation, leadership, and collaboration that E-Cell stands for. The session aimed to familiarize students with the Cell’s vision, showcase the opportunities available, and inspire them to step into the world of entrepreneurship. Highlights The session opened with the introduction of the core team and leads, setting the stage for students to get to know the people who would be driving initiatives throughout the tenure. This was followed by a comprehensive introduction to the various functional teams—including Web Development, Creative, Content, Sponsorship, Media, Research & Development, Operations, and Marketing. Each team’s responsibilities were highlighted, from building digital platforms and designing creatives to managing sponsorships, curating content, driving research, handling events, and promoting E-Cell’s initiatives. This helped the attendees gain a clear picture of how E-Cell operates as a unit and the diverse opportunities available for them to contribute. To make the session more engaging, the official merchandise for the tenure was unveiled, creating excitement and a sense of belonging among the participants. Adding a fun twist, the team conducted an interactive quiz on guestimates, which not only tested logical thinking but also encouraged collaboration and lively participation. Towards the end, the event moved into a Q&A session, where attendees interacted directly with the team to clarify their doubts about E-Cell’s functioning, the recruitment process, and ways to contribute. The session concluded with the roll-out of the recruitment form, opening the doors for new members to join the community and actively participate in upcoming projects and initiatives. ✨ The Orientation concluded on a high note, filled with energy, enthusiasm, and curiosity. It set the perfect tone for a tenure dedicated to innovation, teamwork, and impactful initiatives, while laying the foundation for a stronger and more vibrant entrepreneurial community at IGDTUW.
