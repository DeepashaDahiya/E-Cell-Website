Where ideas spark and founders grow.

We are the Entrepreneurship Cell of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students. We believe in turning ideas into reality, empowering young minds to become future leaders and changemakers.
