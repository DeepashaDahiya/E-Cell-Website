About E-Cell

E-Cell IGDTUW is a student-led initiative that nurtures innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship on campus. We provide a dynamic platform where students can explore groundbreaking ideas, gain mentorship from industry experts, and collaborate with like-minded peers. By fostering leadership and problem-solving skills, we empower students to build impactful solutions that drive positive change in society and prepare them to be future innovators and changemakers.

Our Aim

We aim to be the guiding light that illuminates their path to success, inspiring them to embark on their entrepreneurial ventures with confidence and determination. Our vision is to see our students not just excel academically but to become trailblazers in their respective fields, leading innovative initiatives and ventures that make a positive and lasting impact on society.

Our Vision

E-Cell envisions the creation of a vibrant community of zealous individuals, all sharing a common passion for entrepreneurship. We aspire to be the catalyst that ignites the entrepreneurial spirit within the students of various branches in our university, right from the early stages of their college journey. Our vision is to introduce them to the rapidly evolving world of entrepreneurship and provide them with opportunities to explore diverse entrepreneurial fields.

Our Goal

The goal of the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW) is to foster an entrepreneurial mindset by equipping students with essential skills in innovation, leadership, and problem-solving. To provide a supportive platform that broadens perspectives, encourages creative thinking, and empowers students to transform ideas into impactful ventures that shape the future.
